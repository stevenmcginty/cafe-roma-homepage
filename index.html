Our Story

A warm corner of St Albans, since 1996.

A local fixture for nearly three decades — now with a fresh chapter.

A fresh chapter for a much-loved St Albans cafe — under new ownership since 2024.

Cafe Roma has been part of St Peter's Street since 1996, serving the St Albans community for nearly three decades. In 2024, Velina took over — bringing a decade of experience as a store manager at Costa, and a genuine passion for great coffee and warm hospitality.

Under her care, the cafe has a cosy atmosphere, genuinely warm service, and proper espresso — with fresh energy and small touches that make every visit feel considered. Whether you're stopping in on your walk to work or settling in for the afternoon, you're welcome to stay a while.

How It Works

Order from your table

Scan, order, relax — pay whenever you're ready, even from the seat outside.

Scan · Order · Relax
  1. 01

    Scan the QR on your table

    Every table has a tent card with a QR code. Point your phone — no app to download, no sign-up required.

  2. 02

    Browse the menu & order

    Tap through the full menu, add to your cart, and send it straight to the kitchen. Your favourites save for next time.

  3. 03

    Sit anywhere — we'll bring it

    Inside, by the window, or the pavement tables out front. Your table number goes with the order, so we'll find you.

  4. 04

    Pay & request the bill in-app

    Settle up from your phone whenever you're ready, or tap Request Bill and we'll come over. Points added automatically.
