About Cafe Roma

A warm corner of St Albans since 1996

A St Albans institution since 1996, now with a fresh new chapter.

Cafe Roma has been a beloved fixture on St Peter's Street for nearly three decades, serving the St Albans community since 1996. In 2024, Velina took over the reins — bringing a decade of experience as a store manager at Costa and a genuine passion for great coffee and warm hospitality.

Under Velina's care, Cafe Roma has kept everything you love — the cosy atmosphere, the friendly faces, the perfect espresso — while adding a fresh energy and new touches that make every visit feel special. Whether you're a regular who's been coming for years or you're discovering us for the first time, you'll always feel right at home.
